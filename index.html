FAQ

Common questions

Is this really free? +
Yes, completely free. You can create and download unlimited invoices with no account required. We plan to add premium features in the future, but the core invoice generator will always be free.
Is this invoice valid for ATO purposes? +
Yes. Our invoices include all required fields for a valid Australian tax invoice: supplier name, ABN, date, description of goods/services, GST amount, and total price including GST. This meets ATO requirements for invoices over $1,000.
Do I need to register for GST to use this? +
No. If your annual turnover is under $75,000 you're not required to register for GST. You can set the GST rate to 0% and still create a professional invoice with your ABN. If you are GST registered, our 10% calculation handles it automatically.
Where is my data stored? +
Nothing is stored on our servers. All invoice data stays in your browser only. When you close the tab, the data is gone. We never see your business information or your clients' details.
Can I use this for my Chinese-speaking clients? +
Yes! Use the EN/中文 toggle in the top right to switch the entire interface to Chinese. You can also mix languages — type Chinese in the description fields while keeping the invoice structure in English.
